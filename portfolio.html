Hi, my name is Laura Veiga and I'm from Coimbra, Portugal. I took my degree in Design and Multimedia at the University of Coimbra, where I'm currently taking my master's degree.
As I explained in the beginning of this website, I haven't decided yet which area I want to follow, so I'm on a quest to explore and learn more until I find out which area I like the most (if not all).
During these last few years, I have been involved in the student community in order to hone my design skills, and I ended up being involved in the image development of a literary project.
For now, I'm more focused on learning to improve my design skills, so this website will bring those projects together, which will seem like a big mess, but will be very useful to me in the future... hopefully.
